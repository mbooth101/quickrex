Changing Color- and Font-settings

The background- and foreground-colors for matches in the test-texts as well as the font for the test-text and the regular expression can be changed using the preferences pages. Open the menu Window > Preferences and navigate to Workbench > Colors and Fonts. You should find a Category (denoted by a folder) 'QuickREx', holding the customizable colors and fonts of the plugin as displayed below.
